# CrystalBio Home-Screen Install Instructions

Share these simple steps with the CrystalBio team after sending the app link:

```text
CrystalBio Field App link:
https://work.convogenie.ai

Please open this link on your phone and add it to your Home Screen. After that, use it like a normal app.
```

## For iPhone users

1. Open **Safari** on the iPhone.
2. Go to: `https://work.convogenie.ai`
3. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari.
4. Scroll and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Keep the name as **CrystalBio** or **CrystalBio Field App**.
6. Tap **Add**.
7. Open the app from the new icon on the iPhone Home Screen.
8. When asked, allow:
   - **Location** — needed for check-in/check-out and visit GPS.
   - **Camera/Photos** — needed if attaching visit photos.

## For Android users

1. Open **Chrome** on the Android phone.
2. Go to: `https://work.convogenie.ai`
3. Tap the **three dots** menu in the top-right corner.
4. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**.
5. Keep the name as **CrystalBio** or **CrystalBio Field App**.
6. Tap **Add** / **Install**.
7. Open the app from the new icon on the Android Home Screen.
8. When asked, allow:
   - **Location** — needed for check-in/check-out and visit GPS.
   - **Camera/Photos** — needed if attaching visit photos.

## If the app looks old after an update

1. Close the app fully.
2. Reopen it from the Home Screen icon.
3. If it still looks old, open the link once in Safari/Chrome and refresh.
4. Then open from the Home Screen icon again.
